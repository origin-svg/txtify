# Privacy Policy (Beta)

**Last updated:** March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the application ("the App") handles user information.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App does **not collect, store, or share any personal information**.

Any text entered by the user is processed locally on the device and is not transmitted to any server.

## 2. Data Usage

Since no data is collected, no user data is used, analyzed, or shared.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App does **not use third-party services**, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

## 4. Data Storage

All content created within the App (such as text files) is stored locally on the user's device. The developer has no access to this data.

## 5. Security

While the App does not collect personal data, basic measures are taken to ensure the app runs safely and securely.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App does not target children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal data.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be reflected with an updated date.

## 8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the GitHub repository.

---

**Note:** This is a beta privacy policy and may be updated as the App evolves.
